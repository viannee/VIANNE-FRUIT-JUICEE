About Vianne Fresh Juice

Bringing Naturally Fresh flavors since 2024

Our Story

Founded by Muhangi Vianney with a passion for Naturally Taste, Vianne Fresh Juice began as a small Juice Business in 2020 at Forest Mall Lugogo. Our founders and Customers missed the Naturally made fresh flavors of their homeland and wanted to share them with their new community.

What started as a simple venture quickly grew into a beloved local establishment, known for its commitment to quality Fresh and Natural served with modern convenience.
